We chose the famous tall buildings - Excellence Huang-gang Century Center as our object of design. Located near the Futian CBD, Excellence Huang-gang Century Center consists of 4 buildings, the highest one is as high as 208m, and the lowest one is as high as 180m. It is one of the typical Office buildings in Shenzhen. After the investigate and survey of the people who work in the building, we came up with 14 functions which are needed, and we tried to find out the characteristic of these functions.

The project was inspired by the drum solo Psappha by the Greek composer Xenakis, which was honoring the ancient poetess Sappho, of whom Xenakis credited for introducing shifts into the rhythmic patterns of her poems. He borrowed this metabolic structure for the music which becomes the feature of it. The piece can be divided into 8 sections by their obvious shift in temple and rhythm. This architecture project is based on section 6, which is unique in its division of silence and beats
