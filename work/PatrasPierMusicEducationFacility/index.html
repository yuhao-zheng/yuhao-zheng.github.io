Apply Elements To Project

Connection - Brige

We then translate this analysis of notation from the composer to a set of architectural language.

A. The difference set is used to create cavities for each "sound block" which accommodates the programs.The central points of the each group of difference set of the scores are translated into the center of the cavity.
B. The intersect are used to create both the connections from the ground to upper levels and from one sound block to another.
C. The union set is used to create the light holes which allows light to come into the building and creats unique light effect on the walls.

There are galleries outside both painting and sculpture; the special outdoor classroom sits on the sea provides the students with the best atmosphere of nature and light for their practice, while making the practice a kind of performance for the public who are attracted by the school and the park… The light that penetrates through the heavy concrete, creating unique light effect like the spot light on the stage for the rehearsal rooms and individual practice rooms. The cavities provides acoustical benefits for musical and instrument classrooms.
This educational institute spreads the positive influence of art to the city, and brings people to the water which triggers more creative thoughts which levels up the desire for art in the institute even higher.
